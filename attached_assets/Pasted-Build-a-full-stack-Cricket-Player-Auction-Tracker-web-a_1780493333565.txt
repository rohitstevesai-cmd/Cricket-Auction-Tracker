Build a full-stack Cricket Player Auction Tracker web app (like IPL auction) with two separate dashboards. Use React + Tailwind CSS for the frontend, with localStorage or a simple JSON-based state for persistence (no backend required for MVP).

---

## 🎨 DESIGN DIRECTION
- Theme: Dark luxury sports aesthetic — deep navy/black backgrounds, gold/amber accents, sharp cricket-inspired visuals
- Font: "Bebas Neue" for headings, "DM Sans" for body text (Google Fonts)
- Vibe: Premium, cinematic — like an official IPL broadcast dashboard
- Full responsive design (mobile + desktop)

---

## 🏠 PAGE 1: PUBLIC DASHBOARD (Read-Only)

### Navbar
- App logo/name (e.g. "AuctionX" or "Cricket Auction Hub")
- Two tabs: "Players" | "Teams"
- A hidden trigger: clicking "Privacy Policy / Terms" link in navbar footer **10 times** opens the Management Dashboard (no password required, just 10 rapid clicks — show a subtle click counter toast like "🔒 3/10")

---

### TAB 1: Players List
- A full searchable, filterable, sortable list of all players
- Each player card shows:
  - Photo (avatar/uploaded image)
  - Name, Age, Village/City
  - Player Type badge: Batsman | Bowler | All-Rounder | Wicket-Keeper
  - Additional Tag badge: Normal Player | Captain | Vice Captain
  - Status tag: ✅ Available (green) | 🔴 Sold (red with team name)
  - If sold: show team logo/color and team name on the card

- Filters (sidebar or top bar):
  - By Player Type (Batsman, Bowler, All-Rounder, WK)
  - By Status (Available / Sold / All)
  - By Additional Tag (Captain, Vice Captain, Normal)
  - Sort by: Name A-Z, Age, Recently Added

- Stats bar at top:
  - Total Players | Available | Sold | Teams

---

### TAB 2: Teams List
- Grid of team cards showing:
  - Team logo/image
  - Team name
  - Team location
  - Number of players in the team
  - Click on a team → opens team detail page showing all players in that team with their cards

---

## 🔐 PAGE 2: MANAGEMENT DASHBOARD (Owner Only)

- Accessible by clicking the "Privacy Policy" / "Terms & Conditions" link in the footer 10 times
- Show a subtle floating toast counter as user clicks (e.g. "🔒 7/10 — Keep going...")
- On 10th click: smooth modal/slide-in that reveals the Management Dashboard (no password, just the secret click mechanic)

### Management Dashboard has 3 sections (tabs):

---

### Section A: Manage Players
- Full list of all players with Edit / Delete buttons
- "Add New Player" button opens a form/modal with:
  - Name (text)
  - Age (number)
  - Village / City (text)
  - Player Type: dropdown → Batsman | Bowler | All-Rounder | Wicket-Keeper
  - Additional Tag: dropdown → Normal Player | Captain | Vice Captain
  - Photo: image upload (stored as base64 or URL)
  - Status: Available / Sold
  - Assign to Team: dropdown (optional, pick from existing teams)

- If a player is assigned to a team → their status auto-changes to "Sold" and they get the team tag
- Edit player: same form pre-filled
- Delete player: confirmation prompt

---

### Section B: Manage Teams
- List of all teams with Edit / Delete buttons
- "Add New Team" button opens a form/modal with:
  - Team Name
  - Team Logo / Image (upload)
  - Team Location / City
  - Team Color (color picker — used as accent on team card)
  - Description (optional)

- Delete team: ask if players should be unassigned (set back to Available) or also deleted

---

### Section C: Player ↔ Team Assignment
- A two-panel interface:
  - Left: list of Available players
  - Right: list of Teams
  - Drag a player to a team OR use "Assign" button
  - Each player row has: "Assign to Team" dropdown + Confirm button
  - Each team row shows its current players with "Remove from Team" ✕ button
  - Removing from team → player goes back to Available status

---
